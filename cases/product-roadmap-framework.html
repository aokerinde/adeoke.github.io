01 · Framework

How I structure a roadmap

Every initiative on the board answers three questions before it earns a quarter slot: what outcome does this move (not just what ships), how was it prioritized relative to everything else competing for the same team's time, and what's the return if it's funded. The three tools on this page are the working parts of that framework — a quarter-by-quarter board with a dual audience view, a live RICE scoring calculator, and a payback/ROI calculator for the investment conversation.
